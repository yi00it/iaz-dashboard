Snaplines Plugin Demo

This demo shows the Snaplines Plugin that displays visual alignment guides when dragging or resizing widgets. Try dragging or resizing widgets to see green snaplines appear!

How it works:

  • Green dashed lines appear when widget edges align
  • Lines show alignment with left, right, center, top, bottom, and middle of other widgets
  • 10-pixel snap threshold for smooth alignment
  • Works during both drag and resize operations
